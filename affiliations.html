Computable and Experimental Economics Laboratory (CEEL)

University of Trento · Trento, Italy

CEEL is one of Italy's oldest experimental economics laboratories, known for interdisciplinary research on economic behavior, cognition, and decision making. Its research program brings together economics, cognitive psychology, and organizational studies to examine how human cognition shapes economic behavior, using laboratory, online, and field-based methods. The lab's mission includes translating experimental findings into insights for the design of economic institutions and organizational policies.

I completed my PhD at CEEL and then worked there for three years as a postdoctoral researcher. Altogether, it was my academic home for the first seven years of my career: a formative environment of intellectual training, collaboration, and fun.

Research Collaboration

Viipuri Lab for Behavioral and Experimental Research

LUT Business School · Lappeenranta, Finland

Viipuri Lab (LUT) is a multidisciplinary research laboratory dedicated to studying human decision-making through controlled experiments. The lab brings together researchers from several disciplines, including economics, marketing, strategic management, and finance to examine how individuals behave and make choices in different contexts.

Member · Since 2023

TOM Society

International research community in organization and management

The TOM Society is an international community of organizational scholars dedicated to advancing formal modeling methods in organization and management research. Founded in 2010 by Jerker Denrell and Thorbjorn Knudsen, with intellectual guidance from James G. March, the society has developed around a shared commitment to rigorous theoretical modeling.
